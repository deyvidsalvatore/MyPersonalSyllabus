Welcome to DEV001: Angular Essential Training

Dear Students,

Welcome to DEV001: Angular Essential Training! We are thrilled to have you in this course, where you will gain a solid foundation in Angular and develop the skills needed to build dynamic, modern web applications.

Throughout this course, you will explore the key features of Angular, including components, directives, services, and routing. Our goal is to provide you with both theoretical knowledge and practical experience to help you succeed in your web development projects.

In the Resources section of the course, you will find various materials, including lecture notes, assignments, and links to additional resources. Be sure to check this section regularly for updates and new content.

The course is structured into five units, each designed to build on the previous one. You will find detailed information about each unit in the corresponding links provided. Make sure to complete the readings and exercises as scheduled to stay on track.

For any questions or assistance, please do not hesitate to reach out. We are here to support you throughout your learning journey.
